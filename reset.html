Reset & reinstall your duck

This gives your DeskDuck a fresh copy of its software, straight from your computer. It's the fix-it button.

What this does: It reinstalls your duck's software from scratch, and clears the WiFi network your duck was connected to. Reach for it if your duck is acting buggy, or when you want to move your duck to a new WiFi network or a new home.

It's completely safe, and it won't change your duck's identity — your duck keeps its permanent ID. Afterwards you'll just open the DeskDuck app and set your duck up again like new, including entering your WiFi.

Keep your duck plugged in until it says it's done. 🦆

How to do it, step by step

  1. Use a laptop or desktop computer with the Chrome or Edge browser.
  2. Plug your DeskDuck into the computer with a USB cable.
  3. Click “Reset My Duck” above.
  4. A small window will pop up asking you to pick a device. Choose the one that appears — it may be named something like “USB Serial” or “CP210x.”
  5. Wait for it to finish. Don't unplug your duck until it says it's done.
  6. Open the DeskDuck app and set your duck up again like new, entering your WiFi.

You'll need a computer for this part

Resetting your duck needs a computer (not a phone) using the Chrome or Edge browser. Please open this page there to continue.

What you'll do once you're on a computer

  1. Open this page (deskduck.ca/reset) in Chrome or Edge on a laptop or desktop.
  2. Plug your DeskDuck into the computer with a USB cable.
  3. Click the “Reset My Duck” button that appears.
  4. Pick your duck from the little pop-up (it may be named “USB Serial” or “CP210x”).
  5. Wait for it to finish — don't unplug until it's done.
  6. Open the DeskDuck app and set your duck up again, entering your WiFi.
Resetting is safe and repeatable — you can do it as many times as you need. Your duck keeps its permanent ID; only its software and saved WiFi are refreshed. Questions? get in touch.
