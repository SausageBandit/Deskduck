Reset my duck

This gives your DeskDuck a fresh copy of its software, straight from your computer. It's the fix-it button.

💻

Resetting your duck needs a computer (not a phone) using Chrome or Edge. Please open this page there.

What this does: It reinstalls your duck's software fresh, and clears the WiFi network your duck was on. Reach for it if your duck is acting buggy, or when you want to move your duck to a new WiFi network or a new home.

It's completely safe, and your duck keeps its permanent ID. Afterwards you'll just open the DeskDuck app and set your duck up again, entering your WiFi like new.

This browser can't reset your duck — please use desktop Chrome or Edge. To reset your duck, this page needs to run over a secure (https) connection.

Keep your duck plugged in until it says it's done. 🦆

How to do it, step by step

  1. Use a laptop or desktop computer with Chrome or Edge (this won't work on a phone).
  2. Plug your DeskDuck into the computer with a USB cable.
  3. Click “Reset My Duck” below.
  4. When a small window pops up asking you to choose a device, pick the one that appears (it may be named something like “USB Serial” or “CP210x”).
  5. Wait for it to finish — don't unplug until it says it's done.
  6. Open the DeskDuck app and set your duck up again, entering your WiFi.
Resetting is safe and repeatable — you can do it as many times as you need. Your duck keeps its permanent ID; only its software and saved WiFi are refreshed. Questions? get in touch.
